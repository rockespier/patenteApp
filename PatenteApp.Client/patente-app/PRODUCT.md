# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Driving school students preparing for the Italian driving-license theory exam. They use the app in short study sessions, often on mobile, to practice timed quizzes, review mistakes, and track progress over time.

## Product Purpose

Help learners practice exam-style questions, submit a full simulation under time pressure, review wrong answers, and return to study without losing their place. Success means the app feels reliable, fast to scan, and trustworthy enough for repeated practice.

## Brand Personality

Focused. Practical. Reassuring.

The voice should be clear and direct, with minimal friction and no unnecessary flourish. The emotional goal is to keep students concentrated and reduce anxiety during practice and review.

## Anti-references

Avoid gamified study-app tropes, playful mascots, noisy dashboards, cluttered chrome, and generic SaaS styling. Do not make the interface feel decorative, childish, or over-animated.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the next action obvious at every step.
2. Make review and correction states easier to scan than the main quiz.
3. Prefer calm confidence over visual novelty.
4. Preserve speed and focus on mobile first.
5. Surface progress, mistakes, and time pressure clearly without adding noise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target strong keyboard usability and screen reader clarity. Keep interactive elements labeled clearly, preserve logical reading order, and make state changes understandable without relying on color alone. Maintain high contrast, comfortable tap targets, and reduced-motion behavior for users who need it.
